Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk is a technology entrepreneur and engineer. He is the founder, CEO, and lead designer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., founder of The Boring Company, and co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI.

Key Achievements

  • Founded SpaceX in 2002 to reduce space transportation costs and enable Mars colonization
  • Joined Tesla in 2004 as chairman and lead investor, later becoming CEO
  • Co-founded PayPal (formerly X.com) in 1999
  • Founded The Boring Company to build underground transportation tunnels
  • Co-founded Neuralink, developing brain-computer interface technology

Known for his ambitious goals and innovative approach to technology, Musk has become one of the most influential figures in the tech and business world.
